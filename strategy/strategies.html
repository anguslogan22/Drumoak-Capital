The “Buffett Bounce”

Live soon on Drumoak Capital

Idea: When Berkshire Hathaway discloses a new equity position, the market often responds with follow-on buying. Investors anchor to Buffett’s reputation and implied cost basis, creating short-term momentum that can persist for several sessions.

How it works

  • Signal: Confirmed Berkshire 13F/13G/13D filings or credible news of a new stake.
  • Entry: Systematic triggers after confirmation (no anticipatory trades).
  • Holding Period: ~5 trading days, targeting the initial post-disclosure momentum.
  • Risk Controls: Tight stop-loss, fixed position sizing, and time-based exits.

Recent example

UnitedHealth (UNH): After reports of a Berkshire position, shares bounced from the mid-$200s to above $300 as capital flowed in — precisely the kind of move this strategy seeks to capture.

Carnival Corp (NYSE: CCL) Idea

(Not financial advice — just sharing an idea I’m following)

Key points

  • 42% of global cruise industry passengers
  • 104% occupancy in the first six months of FY2025
  • +9% revenue growth with only +3% cost increase
  • +$609M YoY net income gain in the first two quarters
  • P/E ~17, cheaper than competitors
  • Plans to expand fleet in 2026
  • FY2024 = first profitable year since 2019, similar turnaround to CELH (where Drumoak booked +42%)
  • EBITDA per ALBD set to hit 2026 target one year early

Upside

  • Occupancy and pricing power driving stronger yields
  • Operating leverage as revenue growth outpaces costs
  • Deleveraging + lower interest expense creating equity upside
  • Growth optionality from new fleet and exclusive destinations

Downside

  • High debt: $27.5B still an overhang
  • Macro risks: slower-than-expected rate cuts (though less likely after recent Fed signals)
  • Fuel exposure: spikes could squeeze margins

Drumoak take

Carnival looks like a classic recovery trade: market leader, back to profitability, trading at a discount. With debt still the key risk, execution on bookings, yields, and refinancing will decide how much upside is unlocked.
