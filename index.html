Key Debate

Games Workshop is an exceptional business, but at roughly 28x earnings many investors argue the stock is already fully valued and that Space Marine 2 licensing strength will not repeat.

Drumoak View

The market is missing two things: the core business is still structurally growing, and the Amazon / Warhammer franchise opportunity is being given almost no probability-weighted value despite meaningful upside if execution progresses.
